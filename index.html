Fied Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Egg
  • Canoloa Oil

Add oil to medium hot pan. After pan has heated add vegtables and rice. Let that Sit for a couple minutes stiring frequently. Pour Soy Sauce over pan and let it cook into rice. Two minutes before taking food out of pan add Eggs and let them cook next to the rice beofre taking food out of pan

Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

  • Thawed Chicken
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Canoloa Oil

Bring the pan to medium heat. Add the sliced thawed chicken to the pan and let them cook until they are just starting to change from white. Pour Teriyaki sauce over them and let the sauce cook into the chicken. There Should still be sauce let in the pan once it's done cooking. Pour the remaing sauce and take the chicken out of the pan.

Homemade Udon Noodles

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water

Mix the ingredients until the dough is just a little bit sticky. Let the dough sit and relax for at least 20 minutes. Roll the dough out to a quarter inch and slice it into noodle size strips. Boil The noodles for 5 minutes and serve.
